## 4. Risks and Blockers

- **EMC test failure** — If emissions exceed limits, fix cycle adds 2–4 weeks
- **Safety test failure** — Hipot or temp rise failure requires design change
- **Thermal cycling** — Solder joint cracking possible during −30°C to +55°C cycling
- **Environmental equipment** — Vibration table and thermal chamber availability
