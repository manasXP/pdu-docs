## 4. Risks and Blockers

- **Solder defects** — Cold joints on D2PAK power devices require rework under microscope
- **TIM application** — Incorrect compression can degrade thermal performance
- **Mechanical fit** — First build may reveal tolerance issues with heatsink mounting
